What I Like To Do For Fun

When I'm not studying or working, I like to take time off to expand my horizons by learning something new. Personally and professionally, I'm very curious and I have a deep belief that creating something is one of most beautiful things we can do as humans. Some of my interests include music and photography.

Music

People say that music saves lives and to some extent, that's true. When life get's stressful or I'm navigating new chapters in my life, music has been constant through it all. I like to experience all of music by listening, playing and meeting the creators behind it.

I like to explore new music and to share what I've found. I don't have a favorite genre, so I listen to a wide range of music on a monthly basis. Have a listen!

I bought my current (and first ever) guitar during my sophomore year in highschool. From there I taught myself how to play and have been improving ever since. #closetjamz is a segment I started on my Instagram to showcase what I've been learning.

Thankfully, I was able to attend Van's Warped Tour's Final Full Cross National Tour in 2018. While I was there, I met Waterparks who is one of my biggest inspirations in life and in my design work.

Photography

My dad was a huge photographer (and still is) and wanted to teach someone in the family how to use all of his equipment. I decided to learn by taking a class and practicing during trips. Eventually it grew into a hobby where I would try to cature everything I saw. The most fun part is having the ability to edit them and add my own style later.

One day I asked my twin sister if I could do a practice photoshoot with her and we decided to do it based on the sunset.

My oldest sister and I drove to Austin for a day trip and decided to have an impromptu photoshoot at Zilker Park.

During Halloween in 2018, my friends and I dressed up for the holiday for fun. My friend Adreanna dressed up as Waldo and my twin sister dressed up as Boo from Monsters Inc..
